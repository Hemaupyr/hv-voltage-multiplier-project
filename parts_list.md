- HV capacitors (10–33nF, ≥15kV)

## Protection
- 10MΩ resistors (bleeder chain)
- Silicone HV wire
- Acrylic base
- RTV silicone insulation

## Tools
- Multimeter
- Soldering iron
- Heat shrink tubing
